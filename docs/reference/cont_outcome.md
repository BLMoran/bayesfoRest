# Studies Comparing Truncal Blocks on Pain Outcomes

Outcome data from randomised controlled trials comparing truncal blocks
(eg TAP blocks, rectus sheath blocks, perperitoneal blocks) against
comparator analgesia or placebo in pain outcomes for patients undergoing
midline laparotomy.

## Usage

``` r
cont_outcome
```

## Format

A data frame with 12 rows and 15 variables:

- Author:

  Study author

- Year:

  Year study published

- Subgroup:

  Intervention subgroup

- Mean_Control:

  Control group pain score mean

- SD_Control:

  Control group pain score standard deviation

- N_Control:

  Number of patients in the control group

- Mean_Intervention:

  Intervention group pain score mean

- SD_Intervention:

  Intervention group pain score standard deviation

- N_Intervention:

  Number of patients in the intervention group

- D1:

  Domain 1 of the Risk of Bias 2.0 tool

- D2:

  Domain 2 of the Risk of Bias 2.0 tool

- D3:

  Domain 3 of the Risk of Bias 2.0 tool

- D4:

  Domain 4 of the Risk of Bias 2.0 tool

- D5:

  Domain 5 of the Risk of Bias 2.0 tool

- Overall:

  Overall risk of bias
