# Studies Comparing Medications on Post-Operative Nausea and Vomiting

Outcome data from randomised controlled trials comparing anti-emetic
medications (eg ondansetron, dexamethasone) against comparator
anti-emetics or placebo in PONV outcomes for patients undergoing
surgery.

## Usage

``` r
binary_outcome
```

## Format

A data frame with 12 rows and 18 variables:

- Author:

  Study author

- Year:

  Year study published

- Subgroup:

  Surgery subgroup

- Control:

  Control intervention

- Intervention:

  Intervention medication

- N_Total:

  Total number of patients in the study

- N_Control:

  Number of patients in the control group

- N_Intervention:

  Number of patients in the intervention group

- Event_Control:

  Number of patients in the control group with the outcome (PONV)

- Outcome_Control_No:

  Number of patients in the control group without the outcome (PONV)

- Event_Intervention:

  Number of patients in the intervention group with the outcome (PONV)

- Outcome_Intervention_No:

  Number of patients in the intervetion group without the outcome (PONV)

- D1:

  Domain 1 of the Risk of Bias 2.0 tool

- D2:

  Domain 2 of the Risk of Bias 2.0 tool

- D3:

  Domain 3 of the Risk of Bias 2.0 tool

- D4:

  Domain 4 of the Risk of Bias 2.0 tool

- D5:

  Domain 5 of the Risk of Bias 2.0 tool

- Overall:

  Overall risk of bias
